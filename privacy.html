Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

<!-- Privacy Policy -->

Introduction

At LakLiech Devs, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our mobile applications.

Please read this privacy policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data, such as names, emails, and social handles, primarily for the following purposes:

  • To manage our affiliate program
  • To respond to contact inquiries
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To send relevant communications about our products and services

We collect this information only when you voluntarily provide it to us through forms on our website.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Affiliate tracking and commission payments
  • Communication regarding your inquiries or projects
  • Improving our website and services
  • Sending occasional newsletters or updates (if you've opted in)

We will never sell your personal information to third parties or use it for purposes other than those stated in this policy.

Security Measures

We implement standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure data storage practices

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and affiliate tracking. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us analyze website traffic and improve your experience.

You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies through your browser settings. However, if you disable cookies, some features of our site may not function properly.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Firebase, and EmailJS. These services may collect information sent by your browser as part of their functionality. Their use of this information is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to rectify inaccurate personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to restrict processing of your personal data
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We recommend checking this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy or to exercise your rights regarding your personal data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@lakliechdevs.com

Or visit our contact page to send us a message.
