Affiliate Terms and Conditions

1. Program Participation

By joining the LakLiech Devs Affiliate Program, you agree to promote our apps in an ethical and legal manner. We reserve the right to terminate any affiliate account for violations of these terms.

2. Commission Structure

Affiliates earn 5% commission on each app download that occurs through their unique affiliate link. Commissions are calculated based on the app's purchase price.

3. Payment Terms

Payments are processed monthly for all earnings that exceed $20. Payments are made via PayPal to the email address provided during registration.

4. Prohibited Activities

Affiliates may not engage in spam, false advertising, or any deceptive practices. Violation will result in immediate termination and forfeiture of unpaid commissions.

5. Cookie Duration

Our affiliate tracking cookies last for 30 days. If a user clicks your link and downloads the app within this period, you will receive credit for the sale.

6. Modifications

LakLiech Devs reserves the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued participation in the program after changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
